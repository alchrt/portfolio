Actionable Insights

With this project, I wanted to focus on three questions around some general ideas:

  • the cost of performance
  • the value of engagement
  • and how to interpret "the clicks".

I used visuals like scatter plots for an in-depth analysis — including as interactive tooltips — or buttons to quickly switch between average and median values, exposing outliers and granting some additional insight.

Click on the View average or View median button to enable parameter change and explore the engagement data. Hover over the bars corresponding to social media platforms in different sites to see additional analysis in your tooltip.

This dashboard was my entry to the June 2024 DataDNA challenge organized by OnyxData on LinkedIn, and was made in Power BI with the additional vector elements designed using Figma.

It's fully interactive — feel free to do a deep dive of your own!
